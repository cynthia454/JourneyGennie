Casa Generosa

Located in Baguio, 700 metres from Mines View Park, Casa Generosa provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a terrace and a bar. Featuring a shared lounge, the hotel is close to several noted attractions, around 700 metres from SM City Baguio, 1.2 km from Burnham Park and 500 metres from Baguio Cathedral. The accommodation features a shared kitchen, room service and organising tours for guests. Guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. With a private bathroom equipped with a bidet and slippers, rooms at Casa Generosa also offer free WiFi, while selected rooms are equipped with a balcony. At the accommodation each room is fitted with bed linen and towels. Camp John Hay is 2.7 km from Casa Generosa, while Lourdes Grotto is 3.5 km away. The nearest airport is Clark International Airport, 159 km from the hotel.
