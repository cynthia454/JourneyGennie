Siargao Sugba Lagoon And Magpupungko

The scenic Sugba Lagoon is the perfect place to spend the day while vacationing on Siargao Island. With a private guide, head to the lagoon where you can enjoy several activities including kayaking, snorkeling, paddle boarding, and bamboo rafting. You also visit the islands of Kauhagan and Pamomo-an, right in the lagoon, for more swimming and sunbathing.
