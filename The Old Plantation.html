The Old Plantation

A wide choice of local and international dishes and snacks are available. Meals may be accompanied by our fine, but affordable wines, and real coffee. The Old Plantation has a relaxed atmosphere, friendly and attentive staff, and a timeless Jazz-flavoured ambience. You may choose to dine on our open terrace or al fresco in the garden overlooking the sea. Alternatively, enjoy a bottle of wine, or a cocktail or two, at our fully-stocked, breezy open-air bar. Currently, we are open every afternoon and evening for Happy Hour (4:30 pm to 6:00 pm - Happy Hour discounts apply) and, after the sunset, you are welcome to stay on for dinner (last orders around 8:00 pm). Lunch is available on weekends or by reservation on weekdays. Located at Island View Beachfront Resort, right on the beach at Barangay Bacong, Anda. For your safety, health and distancing protocols are in place, and all dining is open-air.
